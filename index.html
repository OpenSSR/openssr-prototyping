Open Social Science Research

OpenSSR is a new free and open library of social science research.

Authors can freely upload papers. Readers can freely download them. Anyone can freely subscribe to email updates by topic, author, and more. OpenSSR is operated by a non-profit organization, and it runs on modern open-source software.
